Channel.reset_fx¶

Channel.reset_fx(self)¶

Resets the state of all effects on this channel.

Returns

success

Return type

bool

Raises

Bass4Py.exceptions.BassUnknownError – Some other mystery problem!

This method flushes the internal buffers of the effects. Effects are automatically reset by set_position(), except when called from a “mixtime” Bass4Py.bass.Sync.callback.
